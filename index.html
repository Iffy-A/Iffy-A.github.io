• Ludum Dare 43 - Acolyte •

Acolyte was made during Ludum Dare 43 with the theme being 'Sacrifices Have Consequences'. In Acolyte you move around trying to find certain objects using hints that are spread around the rooms and to ultimately make a sacrifice upon the altar. My role during this game jam was the UI programmer, I was responsible for making sure that the hints appeared on the screen so that the player can see what the hints are. I also had a minor part in other parts such as making sure that the player is able to start and end the game.

• Ukie Game Jam - Neon Gravity Shifters •

Neon Gravity Shifters was made during the Ukie Student Game Jam and the theme was 'Changes'. In Neon Gravity Shifters the player will make sure to avoid all obstacles by using the spacebar to change the gravity and to try and go as far as they can. During the production of the game my role was to provide assets for the game.
